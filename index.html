Who We Are

Glasgow's Hydrogen Racing Team

UG Hydrogen Fuel Racing (HFR) is the University of Glasgow's pioneering motorsport society, building hydrogen fuel cell vehicles to compete on both land and sea.

We're engineers, designers, marketers and entrepreneurs — united by a shared belief that the future of motorsport is zero emission.
