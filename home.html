MISSION STATEMENT

We aim to push the boundaries of sustainable motorsport by providing students with hands-on experience in hydrogen fuel cell technology. We focus on rigorous engineering, safety protocols, and cutting-edge aerodynamic design.

Through our work, we strive to accelerate the transition to clean energy in the automotive industry and inspire the next generation of engineers.

WHAT WE DO

Our team designs, builds, and races high-performance, hydrogen-powered prototype vehicles. We tackle complex challenges involving fuel cell integration, custom powertrain development, and lightweight chassis manufacturing.

Competing on international tracks, our student-led team turns innovative clean-tech concepts into track-ready reality.
