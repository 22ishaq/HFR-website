Land Division

Shell Eco-Marathon

Our land team designs and builds an ultra-efficient hydrogen-powered vehicle to compete in Shell's world-famous Eco-Marathon — a global challenge to see which student team can travel the furthest distance on the least amount of energy.

The vehicle combines lightweight chassis design, advanced hydrogen fuel cell systems, and aerodynamic optimisation to achieve extraordinary energy efficiency figures.

Sea Division

Monaco Energy Boat

Our sea team builds a hydrogen fuel cell-powered boat to compete in the prestigious Monaco Energy Boat Challenge — racing on the sparkling waters of the Mediterranean in one of the world's most iconic locations.

Teams are judged on speed, energy efficiency, technical innovation, and sustainability. This is engineering at its most spectacular.
