About HFR

Who We Are

Glasgow's student-led hydrogen fuel racing team — engineering the zero-emission vehicles of tomorrow, competing on the world stage today.

Our Mission

Racing Towards a Hydrogen Future

UG Hydrogen Fuel Racing (HFR) is the University of Glasgow's pioneering motorsport society, developing hydrogen fuel cell vehicles for competition on both land and sea.

Founded by a passionate group of engineers and entrepreneurs, we exist to prove that performance and sustainability aren't opposites — they're the same destination.

We compete at the highest levels of student motorsport while giving members real-world engineering, business, and marketing experience that sets them apart in industry.
